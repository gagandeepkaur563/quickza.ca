What We Do

A Smarter Way to Deliver

Quickza is a third-party delivery solution designed to help restaurants like yours offer fast, reliable deliveries—without the hassle of managing your own fleet. When your customer asks for delivery, you don’t have to say no. Quickza lets you keep that sale, delight your customer, and even open a new revenue stream—all at a minimal cost.

    Whether you have your own drivers or none at all, Quickza fills the gaps:
  • No need to schedule drivers around busy or slow periods
  • No more overstaffing or paying hourly wages unnecessarily
  • Use us only when you need us. Simple.

How It Works

Flexible, On-Demand Delivery at Your Fingertips

  1. Your Customer Places an Order: When a customer calls or orders for delivery, you simply forward the request through our software.
  2. We Dispatch a Driver: You choose from available time slots and our dispatch team sends out a trained, professional driver immediately.
  3. We Handle the Delivery: Our drivehow rs arrive with thermal bags and take care of all types of payments—cash, card, or prepaid.
  4. You Get Daily Reports: Track everything—deliveries, transactions, and earnings—in one easy dashboard.
